## Why these are public domain

- **Darwin, _On the Origin of Species_ (1859)** and **Strunk, _The Elements of
  Style_ (1918)** were published in the United States before 1929, so they are
  in the US public domain by age.
- **Einstein, _Relativity_**: the German original (1916) is public domain by
  age; the bundled text is Robert W. Lawson's **1920 English translation**,
  also published before 1929 and therefore in the US public domain. (Using a
  pre-1929 translation matters — a modern translation would carry its own
  copyright.)

Project Gutenberg transcribes public-domain works; for readers in the United
States the transcriptions carry no additional copyright. See
<https://www.gutenberg.org/policy/permission.html>.
